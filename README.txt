Add nice looking social share bar to your Wordpress site. 12 designs to choose from.

== Description ==

This plugin adds **beautiful social share bar** to your Wordpress site. You can switch between **12 elegant designs** including 3D.

Stylish Social Share is super easy to install and configure.

It supports most popular social networks and is fully responsive.

More [screenshots and live sample](http://www.wpmaniax.com/stylish-social-share/) you can see on plugins' home page.

<h4>Main Features:</h4>

*	Easy to install and configure
*	12 Nice and elegant designs to choose from
*	Most popular social networks supported (Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, LinkedIn, Reddit )
*	Fully responsive
*	Show before/after content or both
*	Shortcode available to place the Stylish Social Share bar inside your posts/pages
*	Configure where to show (posts, pages, home, archives)


== Installation ==

1. Upload `stylish-social-share` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings / Stylish Social Share to configure the plugin
